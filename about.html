About PEM PORTAL

Welcome to PEM PORTAL, a cutting-edge university document management system designed to revolutionize the way documents are managed in academic institutions. Developed by Paul Ejime Mormah, a BSc Information Technology student at the National Open University of Nigeria, this project aims to provide a secure, efficient, and user-friendly platform for managing documents.

Our Mission

At PEM PORTAL, our mission is to provide a reliable and efficient document management system that meets the needs of universities and other academic institutions. We strive to deliver a platform that is easy to use, secure, and scalable, enabling users to manage their documents with ease.

About the Developer

I am Paul Ejime Mormah, a BSc Information Technology student at the National Open University of Nigeria, Department of Computer Science. I developed PEM PORTAL as my final year project, under the supervision of Dr. Olufemi Folorunsho. I am passionate about technology and innovation, and I believe that PEM PORTAL has the potential to make a significant impact in the academic community.
